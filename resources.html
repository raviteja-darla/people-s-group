Ports Operations/ Stock Holding.
We actively operate through major ports including Visakhapatnam, Vizag, Gannavaram, Krishnapatnam, and other strategic locations, supported by a strong network of channel partners and distributors. Our well-coordinated port operations enable us to maintain efficient handling, storage, and dispatch of cargo. At any given point, we maintain over 100,000 tons of coal stock across various grades, ensuring consistent availability and timely delivery to meet customer demands.
Business In-Sight & Stratergies.
With the strong support of our channel partners, we maintain real-time updates on incoming vessel movements, allowing us to plan our stock holdings efficiently and stay ahead in operations. Drawing on years of industry experience, we closely monitor both international and domestic trends in coal and shipping markets. This insight enables us to make informed forecasts and provide timely recommendations to our valued customers regarding their procurement strategies. We also keep our clients regularly informed about fluctuations in the coal index, helping them make smarter purchasing decisions.
Quality Checks & Inspections.
To ensure complete transparency and customer satisfaction, we encourage bulk buyers to visit the port and personally verify the quality and quantity of the available stock. Upon request, we also facilitate independent third-party inspections to validate the material's specifications. Our commitment to maintaining high quality standards ensures that clients receive consistent, reliable products every time.
Logistics & Supply.
