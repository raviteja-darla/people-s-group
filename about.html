About Us
Who We Are
At PEOPLES GROUP, we have ethically built our legacy since 2018, beginning as a proprietary firm and evolving into a dynamic force in coal trading. We specialize in sourcing and supplying Indian, Indonesian, Russian, U.S., and South African coal, serving diverse industrial segments with unwavering dedication to quality and value.
Our strength lies in delivering competitively priced, high-quality coal products while exceeding client expectations through strategic management, entrepreneurial teamwork, and a customer-first mindset. Headquartered in Visakhapatnam, our operations are supported by a skilled team that oversees sourcing, distribution, and quality assurance across multiple ports.
Driven by a vision to become a leading coal importer and supplier, we are committed to sustainable growth, ethical practices, and long-term partnerships that fuel energy needs and power industrial progress.
Our Services
We offer a comprehensive suite of services tailored to the energy and industrial sectors:
>> Coal Trading: Direct supply of Indian, Indonesian, U.S., Russian, South African, and Australian coal, tailored to meet retail, bulk, and CIF (Cost, Insurance, and Freight) needs.
>> Manganese Ore & Cooking Coal: Reliable sourcing and delivery of industrial-grade ores.
>> Port Operations & Stockholding: Efficient management across ports such as Vizag, Krishnapatnam, and Gannavaram, with stockholding capacity exceeding 1 lakh tonnes.
>> Logistics & Distribution: End-to-end delivery solutions via rail, road, and sea routes, ensuring timely fulfillment across India.
>> Quality Control & Inspections: On-demand third-party inspections and client-supported port visits to verify stock and material quality.
>> Market Intelligence: Advisory on procurement planning backed by real-time updates on coal indices, vessel movements, and global trends.
Experience
Our years of experience are our strongest asset. With deep insights into international and domestic coal markets, we proactively track industry trends, shipping movements, and pricing indices. This enables us to make informed recommendations to our clients, helping them optimize procurement decisions and manage energy supply efficiently.
Our established network of channel partners and distribution allies amplifies our ability to respond to market demands quickly and precisely, ensuring business resilience and reliability.
Accountability
At PEOPLES GROUP, accountability is foundational to every relationship we build. We uphold transparency through open client communication, provide regular updates on stock movements, and encourage quality validations through inspections.
Our operations are guided by a firm commitment to trust, performance, and professionalism—ensuring our customers receive exactly what they’re promised, when they need it.
